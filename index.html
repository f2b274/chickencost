Coop Architecture

A coop requires a bare minimum of 4 - 5 square feet of interior space per bird. Ignore this metric, and overcrowding will lead to stress-induced cannibalism and total flock loss.

Unlock Pro Schematics →

Breed Selection

Large-combed Mediterranean breeds suffer severe frostbite in sub-zero winters. Select heavy-bodied Rose or Pea comb breeds to maintain egg production.

Access Pro Climate Data →

Feed Conversion Ratios

Open troughs waste 15% of your feed to lateral spillage and rodents. Switch to elevated PVC port feeders to immediately reduce your monthly operational overhead.

Use Pro FCR Calculators →

Predator Mitigation

Chicken wire is for containment, not protection. Raccoons easily tear through it. Use 1/2-inch welded hardware cloth secured with screws and fender washers.
